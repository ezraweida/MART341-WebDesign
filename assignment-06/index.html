Bourbon Old Fashioned

Ingredients

Bourbon

  • 2 ounces Cold Buffalo Trace Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey

The Rest

  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 tsp water
  • Garnish: orange peel

Steps

    Get a cold whiskey glass and a sphere ice mold

The Mix

  1. Add the sugar and bitters to a rocks glass, then add water, and stir until sugar is nearly dissolved.
  2. Fill the glass with the large ice sphere, add the bourbon, and gently stir to combine.
  3. Express the oil of an orange peel over the glass, then drop in.
  4. Enjoy the smooth taste of excellence.
