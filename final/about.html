About Us

 

 
Do you know what it takes to be a ball boy? It takes diligence, focus and effort. Here at the University of Montana we not only do our job well but we have a great time doing it! If you think you have what it takes, we want you!
The amount of work required to become a ball boy is not overwhelming or outragous. A usual week will require attending four practices and then showing up on gameday bright and early.
The best part of the job is definitely gameday. You get a front row seat to the best show in Montana. You aso get to hear and experience being a ball boy in the Mecca of FCS Football, an eperience unlike any other.
